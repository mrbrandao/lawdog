# Lawdog — AI Legal Assistant

Lawdog is a plugin for AI coding agents that provides skills and tools tailored to Brazilian legal workflows, especially for individuals navigating civil court proceedings without a lawyer.

## Available skills

- **video2forum** (`/lawdog:video2forum`): Converts video evidence (.MOV, .MP4, etc.) to WebM format accepted by PROJUDI/TJPR court upload systems.

## When to activate

Use this module when the user is:
- Preparing or uploading evidence to a Brazilian court system (PROJUDI, TJPR, e-SAJ, etc.)
- Working with legal documents, petitions, or procedural filings
- Asking about Brazilian civil procedure, neighborhood law (direito de vizinhança), or condominium disputes

## General guidance

- Always prioritize factual accuracy over legal opinion
- Reference specific articles of the Código Civil or CPC when relevant
- Keep responses objective and procedural — the agent is a tool, not a lawyer
- Never mask errors from scripts or tools; surface raw output so the user can act on it
